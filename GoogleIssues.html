Google Issues mailing list

About google-issues

This is the mailing list for distribution of Google Issues postings. Archives are available at the Google Issues Archive. More details about the Google Issues mailing list are available at the full announcement. This mailing list is moderated by Lauren Weinstein.

Using google-issues

To post a message to all the list members, send email to google-issues@vortex.com.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.

Subscribing to google-issues

Subscribe to google-issues by filling out the following form. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you. This is a hidden list, which means that the list of members is available only to the list administrator.

(You may enter a privacy password below. This provides only mild security, but should prevent others from messing with your subscription. Do not use a valuable password as it will occasionally be emailed back to you in cleartext.)

(If you choose not to enter a password, one will be automatically generated for you, and it will be sent to you once you've confirmed your subscription. You can always request a mail-back of your password when you edit your personal options.)
