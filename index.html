About

Vortex Technolgy is a website operated by Lauren Weinstein with the goal of educating others on natural disasters that can affect the city of Los Angeles. Also on this site, you can find access to information provided by Lauren on his blog and Fact Squad Radio.

Possible Natural Disasters

California is prone to earthquakes because it sits upon the San Andreas fault line. Earthquakes are caused by sudden movements of Earth's crust.

Caused by draught within California, wildfires can burn down small towns and hundreds of acres of land. Many fires are started based on human mistake.

Tsunamis are large waves that crash into the coastline and are commonly taller than ten feet in height. These giant waves are created by earthquakes under water that create the rippling affect of these giant waves. Thankfully, these waves are quite rare in Southern California.
